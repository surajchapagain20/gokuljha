Welcome to NepalLife Network!

This is NepalLife , a leading Insurance Company in Nepal having more than 200 branches spread across the nation. The company has its own data center located at its corporate office. We are connected through secured VPN across our branches and through Internet across sub-branches. We have developed our own in-house web application for overall working of our company. We are in a process to locate our DR site at one of the most reliable data center in Nepal. Currently we have located our DR site at our own premise in Kathmandu Valley.

We have two link primary and secondary as a failover from our ISP and our server operates 24*7. Currently we are shifting our server in the virtual environment through VMWare. We have separate database server with failover clustering. We have our own Exchange server for email system. We have dedicated web server that handles the request from our clients all over.All our system and server are secured through Eset NOD32 Anti-Virus.
